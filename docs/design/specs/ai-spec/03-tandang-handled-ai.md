## 3. Tandang-Handled AI

The following capabilities are **owned and operated by the Tandang team** and not specified here:

- **Vector embeddings & semantic search** — Tandang maintains embedding indices for seeds, discussions, tasks
- **Real-time notifications & activity feeds** — Tandang streams updates to UI
- **Consensus voting & coin flip resolution** — Tandang executes voting logic
- **Financial calculations** — Tandang manages Galang target tracking, disbursement
- **Audit logs & blockchain records** — Tandang maintains immutable activity history
- **Geographic indexing** — Tandang maintains location-based seed discovery
- **User role & permission checking** — Tandang enforces community roles
